RiffStream — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

RiffStream ("we", "us") is a one-person studio that sells digital products (stream overlays, sound-effect packs and spreadsheet tools) and operates a small application that publishes our own marketing images to our own social accounts. This page explains what data we handle and how.

1. Who we are

RiffStream is operated by an independent creator. Contact: juancruzeiriz@gmail.com. Our storefronts are etsy.com/shop/RiffStream and jeiriz.gumroad.com.

2. The RiffStream Pinterest application

We run an internal tool that connects only to our own RiffStream Pinterest business account to publish Pins of our own product images on a schedule. This application:

  • does not read, collect, store or process data belonging to any other Pinterest user;
  • does not ask other people to log in or grant access;
  • uses an access token tied to our own account, stored privately on our own server and never shared with third parties;
  • only performs actions we initiate on our own content (creating Pins).

Because the app acts solely on our own account, it does not gather personal data from any third party through the Pinterest API.

3. Information from customers

When you buy a product, the marketplace you purchased from (Etsy, Gumroad, itch.io) processes the transaction and shares with us only what is needed to deliver the order — typically your username, order details and, for support, the email you choose to contact us with. We do not receive or store your full payment details; those stay with the marketplace and its payment processor.

4. How we use information

  • To deliver digital products and provide customer support.
  • To answer questions you send us by email.
  • To comply with the terms of the marketplaces and platforms we use.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

5. Third-party platforms

We rely on services that have their own privacy policies, including Pinterest, Etsy, Gumroad, itch.io and Google (for hosting our automation). Your use of those platforms is also governed by their respective policies.

6. Data retention

We keep order and support correspondence only as long as needed to provide support and meet legal/accounting obligations, then delete it.

7. Your rights

You can ask us what data we hold about you, request a copy, or ask us to delete it, by emailing juancruzeiriz@gmail.com. Marketplace data is also subject to the rights the marketplace itself provides.

8. Children

Our products and application are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

Contact for any privacy question: juancruzeiriz@gmail.com
